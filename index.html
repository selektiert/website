About Us

SELEKTIERT_ is a Vienna-based collective dedicated to fostering creative talents and projects in the fields of art, music, and gastronomy.

Our vision is to support young individuals in realizing their creative ideas and taking the leap into visibility – whether it’s bringing their music to the stage, presenting their art to a culture-savvy audience, or executing extraordinary culinary concepts.

We provide not only organizational support but also resources, networks, and strategic advisory expertise. From supplying equipment and managing logistics to offering curatorial and conceptual guidance for events – we create the stage where creativity and passion can shine.

We place great value on carefully thought-out concepts: bringing together the right artists, musicians, chefs, and personalities to craft unique experiences. Our community, comprised of association members and a steadily growing network of venues, partners, talents, and culture enthusiasts, is the heart of our work.
