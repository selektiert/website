ABOUT

SELEKTIERT_ is a Vienna‑based collective at the intersection of music, food, and contemporary culture. We craft events and identities with a clear point of view: bold, useful, and a little bit cheeky. From club nights and kitchen takeovers to visual systems and sounds, our process balances form and function — the vibe is intentional, the details are obsessive.

We collaborate with artists, venues, and brands to create moments that feel inevitable once you’ve experienced them. Strategy first, then design, then the dance floor.
